Beginnings

Patrick Collison was born in Ireland and moved to the United States at the age of 12. He attended Harvard University and graduated in 2009 with a degree in computer science.

Businesses

Patrick Collison founded Auctomatic, a company that sold software to online auctioneers. He also founded Shyp, a company that offered shipping services. Then founding Stripe.
